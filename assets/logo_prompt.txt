A modern minimalist logo for "TensorMesh", a scientific computing library. 

Design features an abstract 3D mesh structure composed of interconnected triangular and tetrahedral elements, 

seamlessly morphing into a neural network tensor pattern. 

The mesh transitions from structured geometric grid on one side to fluid, flowing tensor connections on the other, 

symbolizing the fusion of FEM (Finite Element Method) and Deep Learning.



Color palette: Deep electric blue (#1E88E5) as primary, gradient to vibrant orange (#FF6D00) representing GPU acceleration, 

with subtle purple (#7C4DFF) accents for differentiation/autodiff.



Clean vector style, flat design with subtle 3D depth, suitable for both dark and light backgrounds.

Tech startup aesthetic, professional yet approachable.

No text, icon only, symmetrical composition, centered.
